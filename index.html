Absolute References

Absolute reference (sometimes called External Links)connect to pages in other domains.In the href attribute of the tag the full URL of the page,image, or other resource you would like to link to is specified. One example might be your quest to list some of the greatest bakeries of all time. Since you want to link to the bakeries themselves and not file you wrote, you would use an absolute reference.

Relative References
